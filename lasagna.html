Brenda's Lasagna

Here's what you need:

  • 1 (16 Ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt to taste (no pepper)
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup mayonnaise (no low fat or miracle whip)
  • 1 bag of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Half a bag of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  • 1 bottle of cheap wine for yourself while you cook this

Directions!

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt; pat pan with paper toweli to remove fat. Stir in spaghetti sauce, mayonnaise and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Drink that wine! Brenda says you earned it!
